About VCAPP

A lot of the business, work and social life moved online.
So i built an app that deliever a good solution for this issue.
New video chat application that provide a platform to meet by video for 2 participants.

How It Works?
You just need to enter your name,
Press on "Copy Your ID",
Send your ID to whom you want to connect,
They need to put your ID in their "ID to Call" Box
and
Press the CALL button.
